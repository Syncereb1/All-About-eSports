Educational Esports website proposal outline:

1. Target Audience
Primary Audience: General audiences with little to no background in Esports who are interested in learning about this growing field.

Secondary Audience: Casual gamers, parents of young gamers, and potential Esports fans interested in understanding the basics of the industry.


2. Project Goals
Educate users on the fundamentals of Esports, including its history, popular games, key events, and roles within the industry.

Simplify the concept of Esports for a general audience, providing clear explanations and relatable examples.

Engage users with interactive elements that make learning about Esports easily accessible and enjoyable.


3. Key Features
Homepage: Introduction to Esports and a brief overview of site contents.

What is Esports?: A page defining Esports, explaining its growth, and its significance as an industry.

Popular Esports Titles: Profiles of major games like League of Legends, DOTA 2, Counter-Strike,also with basic gameplay and tournament info.

Esports Events and Tournaments: Highlights of top global tournaments, like The International and League of Legends World Championship, with dates and brief descriptions.

Careers in Esports: Information on different roles in Esports, such as players, casters, coaches, and analysts.

Short trivia: A JavaScript-based quiz on Esports basics to make learning fun and assess users’ knowledge.
